The model consists of an encoder and a decoder, each layer being connectes through residual paths.

The results are.. OK. If the face is from the training set it makes it pretty good, but otherwise.. not. In the notebook, at the end, there is my face, which was not in the training set.

Word2Vec

A torch based implementation of the Word2Vec embedding model, in preparation for IAIO 2024.

The model consists of a encoder-decoder architecture, in which I can train the CBOW and Skip-gram at the same time.

When passing a word to the model, in form of one-hot encodeing, the output should be the context and vice-versa.

So in the latent space, we shoud get a pretty accurate representation of the given word, in relation to the other words in the vocabulary.
